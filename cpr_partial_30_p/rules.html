Rules
  • The game consists of 10 rounds.
  • You will be given 25 efforts in every round and will submit how many efforts to allocate to Activity G.
  • Sharing Rule
    • You will share 30% of your returns from Activity G with the other group members.
    • You will keep the remaining 70% of your returns from Activity G for yourself.
    • This means you will also receive an equal share of the group’s shared returns from Activity G.
    • You will not share any returns from Activity F.
  • The return on Activity G is determined by the group's total effort allocated to Activity G.
    • 20 - 0.1171 × (group's total effort allocated to Activity G) E$ per effort
  • The return on Activity F is 5 E$ per effort.
  • Your earnings for each round will be calculated as follows:
    • Earnings from Activity F
    • Earnings from Activity G
    • Earnings from Group's Sharing
  • After everyone has submitted their decisions, your earnings for the round will be shown to you.
